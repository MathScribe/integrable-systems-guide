# Workshops and seminars

This page will collect workshop, seminar, summer-school, and program pages that contain substantial material on nonlinear dispersive PDE, inverse scattering, Riemann-Hilbert problems, soliton theory, and asymptotic analysis.

## Inclusion criteria

A workshop page is especially valuable if it preserves slides, videos, lecture notes, problem sessions, speaker lists, or detailed abstracts.

## Initial seed targets

- Fields Institute workshops and programs on nonlinear dispersive PDE and inverse scattering
- Summer schools on integrable systems and nonlinear waves
- Program pages from research institutes such as Fields, SLMath/MSRI, ICERM, CRM, Newton Institute, BIRS, CIRM, Hausdorff Center, and Oberwolfach

Workshop pages should be annotated by topic and by the kind of material available, not merely listed by title.
