# Courses

This page will collect university courses and course-like lecture series relevant to integrable systems, inverse scattering, Riemann-Hilbert methods, nonlinear dispersive PDE, Fourier analysis, spectral theory, and finite-gap methods.

## Inclusion criteria

A course page is especially valuable if it contains at least one of the following:

- lecture notes or typed notes;
- homework or problem sets;
- slides;
- reading lists;
- syllabus with a mathematically meaningful order of topics;
- archived versions from multiple years.

## Initial seed targets

- Peter D. Miller Math 557
- Peter D. Miller Math 651
- KIT inverse scattering, Fourier analysis, dispersive PDE, and functional analysis courses
- Terence Tao graduate courses and notes relevant to dispersive PDE
- Monica Visan course materials relevant to nonlinear dispersive equations
- Peter Perry inverse scattering lectures

Each course should eventually have a structured metadata entry and a short annotation explaining where it fits in the learning path.
