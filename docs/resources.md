## Researcher and resource pages / 研究者与专题入口

### Core directions / 核心方向

- [Peter Perry](https://www.ms.uky.edu/~perry/): inverse scattering, NLS/DNLS, DS II, lecture notes.
- [Peter D. Miller](https://websites.umich.edu/~millerpd/): RHP methods, asymptotics, integrable systems, rogue waves.
- [Dmitry Pelinovsky](https://pelinovsky.mcmaster.ca/): DNLS, nonlinear waves, spectral stability, well-posedness.
- [Tom Trogdon](https://faculty.washington.edu/trogdon/): numerical RHP, notebooks, software, computational materials.

### Related background / 相关背景

- [Monica Visan](https://www.math.ucla.edu/~visan/): nonlinear dispersive PDE.
- [Todd Kapitula](https://sites.calvin.edu/tmk5/index.html): spectral and dynamical stability.
- [Mathew A. Johnson](https://matjohn.ku.edu/): nonlinear waves and stability.
- [Peter Olver](https://www-users.cse.umn.edu/~olver/): symmetry methods, PDE, mathematical software.
