# Finite-gap and algebraic-geometric methods

This page is for finite-gap integration, spectral curves, Riemann surfaces, theta functions, Baker-Akhiezer functions, and algebro-geometric solutions of integrable systems.

## Scope

Core subjects include hyperelliptic curves, Abelian differentials, Abel maps, theta functions, Baker-Akhiezer functions, divisor dynamics, finite-gap potentials, Whitham modulation, and algebro-geometric solutions of KdV, NLS, mKdV, and related systems.

## Role in research training

Finite-gap methods are a bridge between integrable PDE, algebraic geometry, asymptotic analysis, and the theory of Riemann surfaces. Resources here should be selected carefully because notation and prerequisites vary widely.

## Candidate seed resources

The first verification pass should include Peter D. Miller, Charles Weibel for background material where relevant, Peter Olver for symmetry and applied geometry perspectives, and selected summer-school or book-draft materials on Riemann surfaces and integrable systems.
